N00BIEX


This website is a student-run initiative to provide notes and other resources for students of CSVTU University. The notes are not affiliated with the university in any way, but they are created by students who are passionate about helping others succeed. The website also provides links to other helpful resources, such as the university's official website, the library, and the student union.

To check the result, students need to have their CSVTU login credentials. They can log in using their username and password. The result will show the student's total marks, grade, CP, credit, SIP, and CPI.
